WHO IS A WEB DEVELOPER?

A web developer is a tech professional who specializes in creating and maintaining websites. They work on both the front-end, which is the part of the website users interact with, and the back-end, which involves server-side functionality and database management. Web developers use languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for front-end development and may use languages like Python, PHP, or Ruby for back-end tasks. They focus on ensuring that websites are functional, visually appealing, and user-friendly. In addition to coding, web developers often collaborate with designers, content creators, and other stakeholders to deliver a seamless online experience. Their role is crucial in the digital world, driving innovation and shaping how we interact with online content.
